Location

Mafia Island Resort is strategically located on Mafia Island, part of the beautiful Maldives archipelago. Known for its untouched natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Mafia Island is a haven for those seeking an authentic and serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The resort's prime location allows you to immerse yourself in the Maldivian way of life while enjoying the utmost privacy.



Accomodation

Our resort offers a range of accommodation options designed to cater to every guest's needs.
